Top Travelling Destinations

Ketapang, a charming town located in the province of West Kalimantan, Indonesia, is blessed with a range of captivating tourist destinations that appeal to travelers seeking a unique and enriching experience.

Cempaka Jaya Village

7 Days

Max People : 5

Cempaka Jaya Village is an excellent destination for those looking to immerse themselves in the authentic charm of West Kalimantan. Visitors can expect to experience warm hospitality, traditional Melayu architecture, and the opportunity to explore the lush surroundings, including nearby rivers and forests.

from $525
Celincing Suka Baru Village

7 Days

Max People : 5

Celincing Suka Baru Village is an excellent destination for those seeking an authentic and cultural experience in this part of Indonesia. It not only offers beautiful sandy beaches but also a mesmerizing mangrove forest. Visitors can explore the mangrove by local fishermen's boats while fishing.

from $700
Sawi Island

7 Days

Max People : 5

Sawi Island, not a vegetable but a small island in Ketapang Regency, offers stunning sandy beaches and diverse marine life. It's accessible by a one-hour boat ride from Tenggar River and provides opportunities for beach activities and snorkeling, along with homestay options to experience local seafood cuisine.

from $600
