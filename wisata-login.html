Cempedak Jaya Tourism Village

Ketapang, Kalimantan Barat

Cempedak Jaya Tourism Village in Kendawangan Kiri Village, Kendawangan District, Ketapang Regency West Kalimantan is a community-based marine conservation tourism village. Every month in 2022, the island is visited by sailing ships from various countries to rest, enjoy the culinary specialties of Cempedak Island, and shop for supplies. In addition, there are also many Indonesian tourists from Central Kalimantan who visit by sea to enjoy culinary offerings and conservation adventures on the island, which is now also one of the leading destinations in Ketapang Regency.
Cempedak Jaya tourist village offers a 100-beach adventure as its main attraction. A dugong and seagrass conservation tourism program, where tourists are invited to participate in patrolling the marine area around Cempedak and Bawal islands, which are thought to be dugong habitats.



For the efforts of Pokdarwis Cempedak Jaya in dugong and seagrass conservation efforts packaged in a marine ecotourism program, this group received the 1st runner up Local Hero award at the 2021 International Ocean Award event organized by BOAT International and the Blue Marine Foundation in the UK. In 2022, Cempedak Island was also designated as one of the Nusantara Maritime Villages by LANAL Ketapang and is one of the stopover points for international sailing ships.
